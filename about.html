Psychology Practice

Evidence-based therapy with a compassionate, human-centered approach.

My Approach

I believe that therapy is a collaborative process. We work together to explore your thoughts, feelings, and behaviors, gently unraveling the narratives that might be holding you back.

My methodology integrates cognitive-behavioral techniques with a humanistic foundation. This means honoring your unique experience while providing practical tools for tangible change.

Areas of Focus

  • Anxiety and Stress Management
  • Depression and Mood Disorders
  • Relationship Issues and Communication
  • Life Transitions and Identity
  • Self-Esteem and Personal Growth
